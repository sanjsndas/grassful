Privacy Policy

Your privacy is very important to us at Grassful Gardening Services. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you engage with our website, services, and communications. We are committed to ensuring that your information is protected and used responsibly.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information in order to provide you with personalized gardening and pruning services efficiently. The information we collect includes:

  • Personal identification information: such as your name, email address, phone number, and mailing address when you fill out contact forms or request services.
  • Transactional information: details about services requested, quotes, payments, and communications.
  • Technical data: including but not limited to IP address, browser type, operating system, and referring URLs to help us improve our websiteâ€™s functionality and user experience.
  • Cookies and tracking technologies: these help us understand your preferences and tailor your visit to our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly for the purposes described below:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our gardening and pruning services tailored to your needs.
  • To communicate with you regarding service requests, scheduling, follow-ups, and customer support.
  • To send you marketing materials, newsletters, special offers, and announcements, only if you have opted to receive them.
  • To analyze website usage and improve our online presence and services.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms and conditions.

3. Legal Basis for Processing Your Data

Where applicable, we process your personal data based on one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Your consent, which you may withdraw at any time.
  • Performance of a contract to deliver services you have requested.
  • Compliance with a legal obligation.
  • Our legitimate interests in improving our services and customer relationships.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or lease your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following situations:

  • With trusted service providers and partners who assist in operating our business and delivering services, under confidentiality agreements.
  • Where required by law or legal process, such as responding to subpoenas or court orders.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Grassful Gardening Services, our employees, customers, or others.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze usage, and personalize content:

  • Essential cookies: necessary for website functionality.
  • Analytical cookies: help us understand how visitors interact with our site and improve performance.
  • Marketing cookies: used to deliver relevant ads and promotions (only with consent).

You can manage your cookie preferences or disable cookies through your browser settings, although this may affect website functionality.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include secure servers, encryption technologies, access controls, and employee training on data privacy.

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee absolute security but continually work to safeguard your data.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by law. When personal data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have certain rights concerning your personal information. Depending on your location, these may include:

  • The right to access and receive a copy of your data.
  • The right to rectify inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • The right to request deletion of your data in certain circumstances.
  • The right to restrict or object to processing.
  • The right to data portability.
  • The right to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below. We will respond to your request in compliance with applicable laws.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services and website are not directed to individuals under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent. If you believe we have collected data from a child without proper consent, please contact us immediately, and we will take appropriate steps to remove the information.

10. International Data Transfers

If you are located outside the country where Grassful Gardening Services operates, please note that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in our home country or other countries where our service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data according to applicable data protection laws.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or our services. We encourage you to review this page periodically for updates. Continued use of our website or services after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
